# Casa Alejandro website direction

## Corrected cultural hierarchy

Casa Alejandro is presented first and clearly as a restaurant of Reus, Catalonia.

1. Catalan language, Reus, local dining culture, Mediterranean cooking
2. Family tables, daily menus, groups, and practical hospitality
3. Roller-hockey history as part of the restaurant and city memory
4. Any Galician connection only as a minor historical detail, never the visual or verbal theme

The public interface defaults to Catalan with an equal Spanish version. English can be added later for visitors, but it should not displace the two operating languages of the restaurant.

## Core idea

**Cuina de sempre. Ànima de Reus.**

The design should feel like a longstanding Reus dining room after a careful renovation: warm parchment, oxblood, walnut, aged brass, and small blue ceramic accents. The graphic system borrows from picture frames, the metal geometry of the facade, printed menus, and curated sporting artifacts.

## Image research and status

The direction was rechecked against the restaurant’s current Instagram profile and recent TapaReus collaborations visible in 2025–2026. These reinforce:

- Burgundy menu graphics
- Seafood, fideuà, cod, squid, prawns, and generous shared dishes
- Catalan captions and active participation in Reus food culture
- Group menus, cellar, seasonal menus, and opening hours as useful content

The current implementation uses generated production artwork that follows those references but does not reproduce third-party social photographs. These images are temporary and should be replaced with licensed, recent photographs supplied or approved by Casa Alejandro before launch.

The hockey-memory image is intentionally an atmospheric still life rather than a fake documentary record. It is visibly labelled as conceptual in the interface until original archive material is supplied.

## Design tokens

| Token | Value | Role |
|---|---:|---|
| Parchment | `#F3EBDD` | Main background |
| Paper light | `#FAF6EE` | Menu surface |
| Oxblood | `#7B2638` | Brand and conversion |
| Walnut | `#251F1A` | Text and history |
| Aged gold | `#C7A24B` | Rules and local warmth |
| Ceramic blue | `#315F73` | Small tableware/action accent |

Typography:

- Fraunces Variable for the editorial voice
- Source Sans 3 Variable for navigation, menus, hours, and controls

## First implementation scope

- Responsive one-page site
- Catalan/Spanish language switch
- Daily/weekend/group menu tabs
- Signature dishes
- Restaurant and roller-hockey history
- Family/group information accordion
- Hours, telephone, map link, and direct reservation call
- Mobile navigation and reduced-motion support

## Content still requiring owner confirmation

- Current ownership wording
- Exact group capacities and group menu PDFs
- Accessibility information
- Allergy procedure
- Licensed current photographs and original archive material
- Preferred booking method beyond telephone
